Cookie Preferences Modal

This is a static example of the cookie preferences modal that appears when users click "Customize" in the cookie banner.

Features

  • Accessible dialog with proper ARIA attributes
  • Focus management and keyboard trapping
  • Category-based consent options
  • Clear visual hierarchy

Cookie Preferences

Functional Cookies

Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website. This data is anonymized.

Marketing Cookies

Allow us to provide personalized content and ads based on your browsing behavior.
